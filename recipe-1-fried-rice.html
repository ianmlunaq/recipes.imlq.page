Fried Rice üçö

Fried rice is a classic Asian dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and sometimes protein like meat or seafood. Soy sauce and other seasonings add savory flavor, while the cooking process creates a contrast between fluffy rice and crispy bits. It's a versatile and delicious meal that can be customized with endless ingredient combinations. If you cook bacon before the fried rice, you can use the bacon fat to cook, then add the bacon back in at the end. Optionally, serve with chili crisp.

Amount to make:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of leftover cooked rice.
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of frozen veggies.
  • 0.5 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 0.5 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 0.5 Tbsp toasted/untoasted sesame oil
  • 1 green onion

Procedure:

  1. Thinly slice green onion.
  2. Scramble eggs in a bit of oil on medium heat.
  3. When eggs are mostly cooked, add in everything else minus the green onions.
  4. Mix thoroughly and cook until frozen veggies are unfrozen.
  5. Serve topped with green onions.
