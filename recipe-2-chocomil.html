Chocomil is basically Mexican chocolate milk that's popular as a quick breakfast. It's a delicious and slightly thicker version compared to regular chocolate milk, with the most basic ingredients being milk, chocolate powder, and a raw egg. I like adding a whole banana, ground cinnamon, and a bit of oatmeal for flavor and to make it a more substantial drink. You can also add greek yogurt or kefir to give it a bit of a tangy flavor.

Amount to make:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 Tbsp chocolate powder (e.g., Chocomilk, Nesquik)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 banana
  • 0.25 cup of oatmeal
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Procedure:

  1. Blend everything together in a blender.
