Champurrado

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 a cinnamon stick
  • 2 oz piloncillo or dark brown sugar
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1 tablet of Mexican chocolate (e.g., Abuelita, Ibarra)
  • 3 1/2 oz of corn masa flour (e.g., Maseca)
Procedure:
  1. Boil 1 cup of water with cinnamon stick and piloncillo until sugar has completely dissolved.
  2. Add milk and chocolate. Mix until chocolate has completely dissolved.
  3. In a separate container, combine 1 cup of water with masa flour and whisk until no clumps remain.
  4. To ensure there are no clumps of masa, filter the mixture through a sieve as you pour it into the hot chocolate.
  5. Whisk the champurrado constantly to prevent the masa from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  6. Heat until it is at your desired consistency, serve, and enjoy!
